About me

Hi, my name is Kaki Kagatan. I’m an up-and-coming front-end developer currently enrolled in the BCIT FWD program. When I graduated high school I took a gap year to work, but enrolled in BCIT’s CST full-time program the following year. The full-time course load was too much for me at the time, so I decided to take the part-time program, CSC. After taking two part-time front-end development courses I was hooked. I soon later applied for the 2024 FWD in-take and that is we're im studying at now.
